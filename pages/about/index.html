about

this website

This website is just my personal space to build stuff and write without worrying about deadlines.

I made this site mostly because I learned how to use Tailwind, partly because I needed a spot to share my photos without compressing the details out of them, and partly because I still have a few college essays left in me. Maybe I'll get bored of this after a month, idk.
